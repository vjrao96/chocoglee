About us


We are a small group of people who make handcrafted chocolates,cookies,pastries and cupcakes.Our aim is to produce chocolates that are safe to consume and healthy as chocolates are good for heart.The ingredients we use to make chocolates are organic.We raise our own cows,use freshly made jaggery,grow our own vanilla pods,strawberries,raspberries,nuts.We process the cocoa beans at our home which are freshly made everyday.Our chocolates are free of sugar as they contain jaggerry hence they are healthy.The dough is also freshly prepared and gluten-free.Our products are free of eggs.We also cater to Vegans by adding soy-milk or almond milk in our products.We also undertake orders in bulk.Anyone can contact us by reaching out to us through instagram,facebook or whatsapp.We believe that everyone should have access to healthy food so our products are affordable.Besides our shop,we planning to open up an online store so that people from all over the world can access our products.
